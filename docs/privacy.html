Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect OAuth tokens for social media platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) solely for the purpose of publishing videos on your behalf.

2. How We Use Information

Tokens are used exclusively to upload videos to your connected accounts. We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

OAuth tokens are stored locally on your machine in JSON files. No personal data is transmitted to external servers beyond the intended social media platforms.

4. Data Deletion

You can disconnect your accounts at any time through the application, which immediately deletes all stored tokens and associated data.

5. Third-Party Services

This application integrates with the following services. Please review their respective privacy policies:

  • Google / YouTube - Privacy Policy
  • TikTok - Privacy Policy
  • Meta / Instagram - Privacy Policy
