Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Content Data: Text prompts and topics you provide for video generation.
  • Account Data: OAuth tokens when you connect social media platforms (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram).
  • Usage Data: Basic usage statistics to improve the Service.

2. How We Use Information

  • To generate video content based on your input.
  • To upload content to your authorized social media accounts on your behalf.
  • To improve and maintain the Service.

3. Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with third-party AI and social media services including:

  • Google (Gemini, Imagen, Veo) - Content generation
  • OpenAI (GPT, DALL-E, Whisper) - Content generation and transcription
  • ElevenLabs - Voice synthesis
  • YouTube, TikTok, Instagram - Content publishing

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally on your server. Generated content (images, audio, video) is stored locally in your designated output directory. We do not transmit your data to any servers beyond the AI services required for content generation.

5. Data Retention

Generated content is stored locally until you delete it. OAuth tokens persist until you revoke platform connections.

6. Your Rights

  • You can revoke social media platform access at any time.
  • You can delete generated content from your local storage.
  • You can request information about what data is associated with your usage.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us through our official channels.
